Gravitorrent Privacy Policy

We collect nothing. Your downloads are your business.

1. Data Collection: None. Zero.

Gravitorrent is designed to work completely locally. We do not track, collect, store, upload, or sell your personal information, torrent metadata, or app usage statistics.

There are no user registration forms, no databases keeping logs of your activity, and no telemetry tracking your clicks.

2. Permissions Used & Why

To function properly, Gravitorrent requests the following device permissions:

  • Local Network Access: Required for the "Orbit Bridge" feature to detect your other active devices running Gravitorrent and transmit magnets directly to them over your local Wi-Fi.
  • Camera Access (Optional): Used exclusively to scan Orbit Bridge QR codes to pair devices quickly. No image data is ever saved or transmitted.
  • Storage Access: Required to save your downloaded files to your device's storage.
  • Notification Access: Used to alert you when a download is complete.

3. BitTorrent Protocol

As a BitTorrent client, Gravitorrent connects you directly to other peers in the swarm. Your IP address will be visible to other peers you connect with, as required by the BitTorrent protocol itself. Team Antigravity does not operate any of these peers or track these connections.

Your files are downloaded directly peer-to-peer. Nothing is stored on Team Antigravity servers.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our privacy protocol, please contact us at: hello@teamantigravity.com
