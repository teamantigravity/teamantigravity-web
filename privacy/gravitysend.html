GravitySend Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

Team Antigravity offers GravitySend for free to make your life easier. We believe apps should just work, without tracking your every move.

1. Local Privacy

Your files are sent directly between your devices over your own Wi-Fi. We never upload your files to the cloud, and they never touch our servers.

2. Advertisements

We use Google AdMob to show ads so we can keep the app completely free to use. Google may collect standard, anonymous device information to show these ads.
