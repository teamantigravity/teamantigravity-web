GravitySend Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2026

Team Antigravity builds GravitySend as a Freemium application. This service is provided at no operational cost and is intended for use as is.

1. Local Networking

File processing occurs entirely peer-to-peer on your local area network. Assets never clear external storage arrays or servers.

We use Google AdMob infrastructure to monetize our traffic framework. Anonymized device metrics may be logged by Google under their target networks.
