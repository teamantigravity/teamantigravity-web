Privacy by design

We don't want your data.

We don't collect, store or sell your information, and we run no servers to do so. The only third party involved is Google AdMob, which serves ads inside the mobile apps.

Zero collection

No analytics SDKs, no telemetry, no profiles. We never see what you transfer or download.

No servers

We operate no backend. There is nowhere for your data to be stored, because we built nowhere to store it.

AdMob, disclosed

Google AdMob shows ads in the mobile apps and may use a device ad identifier. That's the one exception — and we're upfront about it.

You're in control

Reset or limit ad personalization in your OS settings any time. Full details live in each app's policy.
